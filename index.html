The Missing Piece: Cellular Energy

Inside every cell are tiny structures called mitochondria. Their role is to convert nutrients into usable energy.

As we age, mitochondrial efficiency naturally declines. When this happens, the body may:

  • Produce less energy
  • Burn fewer calories at rest
  • Store fat more easily

This means that even a “perfect” diet can feel ineffective if the body’s energy systems are underperforming.
